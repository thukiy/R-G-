# Kartesisches Produkt 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 152, 321, 197]]<|/det|>
Beispiel: A = {1, 2}, B = {3, 4} 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[126, 219, 460, 316]]<|/det|>
\[
\begin{align*}
\cdot & \quad \text{A} \times \text{B} = \{ (1; 3), (1; 4), (2; 3), (2; 4) \} \\
\cdot & \quad \text{B} \times \text{A} = \xi (3; 1), (3; 2), (4; 1), (4; 2)
\end{align*}
\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[48, 377, 199, 420]]<|/det|>
\[
\Rightarrow \mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B} \neq \mathbf{B} \times \mathbf{A}
\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[48, 481, 170, 523]]<|/det|>
Bemerkung: 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[48, 523, 339, 567]]<|/det|>
Falls A ≠ B, dann A × B ≠ B × A 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[48, 632, 546, 680]]<|/det|>
Rechteck R durch kartesisches Produkt beschreiben: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[48, 675, 247, 730]]<|/det|>
\[
R = [a_j; 3] \times [0; 1]
\]
