# Arithmetik 

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 87, 270, 120]]<|/det|>
## Bruchrechnung 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 140, 334, 196]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{a}{b} \leftarrow \text{Zähler} \quad b \neq 0 \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 203, 302, 237]]<|/det|>
### Kehrwert / -bruch : \(\frac{b}{a}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[28, 264, 290, 290]]<|/det|>
- Gewieken / Kürzen : 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 295, 600, 370]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{1}{c} = \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{c}{c} = \frac{a \cdot c}{b \cdot c} \quad \frac{5}{6} \cdot \frac{3}{3} = \frac{15}{18} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[450, 339, 600, 375]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{30}{50} = \frac{3 \cdot 10}{5 \cdot 10} = \frac{3}{5} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[28, 402, 333, 428]]<|/det|>
- Addition / Subtraktion : 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[85, 440, 551, 466]]<|/det|>
### Nenner des Brüches muss übereinstimmen 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[110, 471, 465, 504]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{a}{c} + \frac{b}{d} - \frac{a}{c} \cdot \frac{d}{d} + \frac{b}{d} \cdot \frac{c}{c} = \frac{ad + bc}{cd} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[100, 520, 955, 564]]<|/det|>
→ um gemeinsamer Nenner zu bestimmen, nehmen wir das kleinste gemeinsame Vielfache der Nenner 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[110, 570, 400, 600]]<|/det|>
\[ \text{Bsp} : \frac{3}{7} + \frac{5}{7} - \frac{3+5}{7} = \frac{8}{7} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[180, 606, 576, 639]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{7}{4} + \frac{3}{5} = \frac{7}{4} \cdot \frac{5}{5} + \frac{3}{5} \cdot \frac{4}{4} = \frac{35}{20} + \frac{12}{20} = \frac{47}{20} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[28, 675, 167, 701]]<|/det|>
- Division : 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[90, 707, 375, 740]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{a}{b} : \frac{c}{d} = \frac{a}{b} \cdot \frac{d}{c} = \frac{ad}{bc} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[90, 746, 550, 780]]<|/det|>
\[ \text{Bsp} : \frac{4}{3} : \frac{2}{5} = \frac{4}{3} \cdot \frac{5}{2} = \frac{4 \cdot 5}{3 \cdot 2} = \frac{20}{6} = \frac{3}{2} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[174, 785, 640, 819]]<|/det|>
\[ \frac{a+1}{b} : \frac{a(a+1)}{3b} = \frac{a+1}{b} \cdot \frac{3b}{a(a+1)} = \frac{3}{a} \]
