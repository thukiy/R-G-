<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[114, 180, 624, 198]]<|/det|>
Nicht statische Lösungen durch Trennung der Variablen: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[384, 198, 730, 225]]<|/det|>
\[ y' = x \cdot (1 + y^2) \quad | : (1 + y^2) \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 230, 735, 271]]<|/det|>
\[ \Leftrightarrow \quad \frac{1}{1 + y^2} \cdot y' = x \quad \left| \int_0^x \dots dx \right. \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 272, 498, 312]]<|/det|>
\[ \Rightarrow \quad \int_0^x \frac{1}{1 + y^2} \cdot y' dx = \int_0^x x dx \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 312, 500, 353]]<|/det|>
\[ \Leftrightarrow \quad \int_1^y \frac{1}{1 + y^2} dy = \int_0^x x dx \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 353, 690, 394]]<|/det|>
\[ \Leftrightarrow \quad \left[ \arctan(y) \right]_1^y = \arctan(y) - \frac{\pi}{4} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \left[ x^2 \right]_0^x = \frac{1}{2} \cdot (x^2 - 0) \quad \left| + \frac{\pi}{4} \right. \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 395, 718, 435]]<|/det|>
\[ \Leftrightarrow \quad \arctan(y) = \frac{1}{2} x^2 + \frac{\pi}{4} \quad \left| \tan(\dots) \right. \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 430, 694, 469]]<|/det|>
\[ \Rightarrow \quad y(x) = \tan\left(\frac{1}{2}x^2 + \frac{\pi}{4}\right). \quad \left| \star \right. \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[114, 467, 785, 503]]<|/det|>
Die letzte Umformung stellt keine Äquivalenzumformung dar. Sie ist jedoch umkehrbar, wenn gilt 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 505, 533, 640]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{align*} \Leftrightarrow \quad \frac{1}{2} x^2 + \frac{\pi}{4} < \frac{\pi}{2} \quad \left| - \frac{\pi}{4} \right. \\ \Leftrightarrow \quad \frac{1}{2} x^2 < \frac{\pi}{4} \quad \left| \cdot 2 \right. \\ \Leftrightarrow \quad x^2 < \frac{\pi}{2} \quad \left| \pm \sqrt{...} \right. \\ \Leftrightarrow \quad -\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}} < x < \sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}. \end{align*} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[114, 642, 732, 661]]<|/det|>

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[119, 661, 550, 705]]<|/det|>
