## Trigonometrisches Polynom vom Grad n 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 452, 220, 480]]<|/det|>
\[p: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{C}\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 483, 670, 550]]<|/det|>
\[p(x) = \sum_{k=-n}^{n} C_k e^{ikx} \quad \text{trigonometrisches Monom}\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 561, 214, 590]]<|/det|>
\[\rightarrow e^{ikx} \triangleq t^k\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 601, 333, 631]]<|/det|>
\[\rightarrow e^{ix} = \cos x + i \sin x\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 653, 808, 808]]<|/det|>
\[p(x) = \sum_{k=-n}^{n} C_k (\cos(kx) + i \sin(kx)) \\
= C_0 + \sum_{k=1}^{n} \left[ C_k (\cos(kx) + C_{-k} \cos(-kx) + i C_k \sin(kx) + i C_{-k} \sin(-kx)) \right] \\
= C_0 + \sum_{k=1}^{n} \left[ (C_k + C_{-k}) \cos(kx) + i (C_k - C_{-k}) \sin(kx) \right]\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[125, 795, 691, 822]]<|/det|>
\[\text{da } \cos(-kx) = \cos(kx) \quad \& \quad \sin(-kx) = -\sin(kx)\]

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 852, 280, 882]]<|/det|>
## reelle Darstellung : 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 886, 532, 930]]<|/det|>
\[p(x) = a_0 + \sum_{k=1}^{n} (a_k \cos(kx) + b_k \sin(kx))\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 950, 384, 976]]<|/det|>
\[a_k, b_k \in \mathbb{C} \quad a_0 = c_0\]
