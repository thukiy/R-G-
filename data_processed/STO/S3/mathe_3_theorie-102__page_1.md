## Additionssatz für Mittelwerte 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 50, 899, 88]]<|/det|>
Zufallsvariable \( z = \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \) gegeben, \( x_i \) entweder steige oder disreule 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 92, 260, 118]]<|/det|>
Zufallsvariablen 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 144, 380, 185]]<|/det|>
\[ E(z) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} E(x_i) = E\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i\right) \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[92, 206, 707, 271]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} \text{\(\longmapsto\) linearität:} & \quad E(a_1 x_1 + \ldots + a_n x_n) \\ & \quad = a_1 E(x_1) + a_2 E(x_2) + \ldots + a_n E(x_n) \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[25, 320, 512, 348]]<|/det|>
## Multiplikationssatz für Mittelwerte 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 357, 477, 425]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} \bar{z} &= x_1 \cdot x_2 \cdot \ldots \cdot x_n \\ E(\bar{z}) &= E(x_1) \cdot E(x_2) \cdot \ldots \cdot E(x_n) \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[25, 473, 440, 501]]<|/det|>
## Additionssatz für Vorräumen 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 508, 500, 580]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} \bar{z} &= \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i \\ \text{Var}(\bar{z}) &= \text{Var}(\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \text{Var}(x_i) \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[55, 600, 963, 636]]<|/det|>
\[ \text{dinearität:} \quad \text{Var}(\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i x_i) = a_1^2 \text{ Var}(x_1) + a_2^2 \text{ Var}(x_2) + \ldots + a_n^2 \text{ Var}(x_n) \]
