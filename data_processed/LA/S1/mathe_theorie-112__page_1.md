Rechenregeln : 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 320, 339, 352]]<|/det|>
a) \(\vec{a} + \vec{b} = \vec{b} + \vec{a}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 358, 310, 390]]<|/det|>
b) \(\lambda \cdot \vec{a} = \lambda \cdot \lambda\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 396, 450, 429]]<|/det|>
c) \(\lambda \cdot (\vec{a} + \vec{b}) = \lambda \cdot \vec{a} + \lambda \cdot \vec{b}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 435, 470, 468]]<|/det|>
d) \((\lambda + \mu) \cdot \vec{a} = \lambda \cdot \vec{a} + \mu \cdot \vec{a}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 475, 230, 507]]<|/det|>
e) \(0 \cdot \vec{a} = 0\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[20, 530, 656, 600]]<|/det|>
\[ \text{Bsp:} \quad \begin{array}{l} \bullet \quad (\frac{2}{3}) + (\frac{5}{7}) = (\frac{7}{4}) \\ \bullet \quad 2 \cdot (\frac{1}{3}) - \frac{1}{2} \cdot (\frac{4}{5}) = (\frac{2}{6}) - (\frac{2}{4}) = (\frac{2}{2}) \end{array} \]
