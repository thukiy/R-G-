# Mengenlehre
## Mengenoperationen 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 180, 411, 225]]<|/det|>
Vereinigung: \(A \cup B = \{x | x \in A \lor x \in B\}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 345, 132, 384]]<|/det|>
Beispiel: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 383, 308, 430]]<|/det|>
\[
\begin{align*}
\cdot \{1, 2\} \cup \{2, 3\} &= \{\xi_1, \xi_2, \xi_3\} \\
\cdot \{1, 2\} \cup \{5, 8\} &= \{\xi_1, \xi_2, \xi_5, \xi_8\}
\end{align*}
\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 576, 428, 620]]<|/det|>
Schnittmenge: \(A \cap B = \{x | x \in A \land x \in B\}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 658, 132, 697]]<|/det|>
Beispiel: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 696, 275, 742]]<|/det|>
\[
\cdot \{1, 2\} \cap \{2, 4\} = \{\xi_2\}
\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 740, 283, 787]]<|/det|>
\[
\cdot \{1, 4\} \cap \{3, 5, 7\} = \{\xi_3\}
\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[47, 785, 226, 831]]<|/det|>
\[
\cdot \{2, 3\} \cap \emptyset = \{\xi_3\}
\]
