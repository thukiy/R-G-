Def: Eigentliche Exponentialfunktion 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[110, 47, 280, 78]]<|/det|>
\[f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}^+\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[150, 85, 580, 117]]<|/det|>
\[x \to f(x) = a^x \quad a \in \mathbb{R}^+ \setminus \{1\}\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 147, 444, 177]]<|/det|>
→ nur positive Funktionswerte 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 186, 844, 215]]<|/det|>
→ alle eigentlichen Exponentialfunktionen gehen durch \(f(0) = 1\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 223, 585, 290]]<|/det|>
\[\begin{aligned}
f(x+1) &= a \cdot f(x) \\
&= a^{x+1} = a^x \cdot a^1 = a \cdot f(x)
\end{aligned}\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 303, 600, 333]]<|/det|>
→ \(0 < a < 1\) : streng monoton fallend 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[104, 343, 550, 372]]<|/det|>
a > 1 : streng monoton steigend

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[104, 440, 360, 472]]<|/det|>
a) \(\sin h : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[202, 468, 530, 511]]<|/det|>
\[x \to \sinh(x) = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2}\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[104, 519, 360, 550]]<|/det|>
b) \(\cos h : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[202 555, 530, 597]]<|/det|>
\[x \to \cosh(x) = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2}\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[104, 600, 460, 631]]<|/det|>
c) \(\tanh : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R} - \{1, 1\}\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[202, 628, 530, 671]]<|/det|>
\[x \to \tanh(x) = \frac{\sinh(x)}{\cosh(x)}\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[104, 675, 536, 707]]<|/det|>
