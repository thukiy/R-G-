## 7. Standardnormalverteilung 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 459, 816, 495]]<|/det|>
U sei eine standardnormalverteilte Zufallsvariable. Berechnen Sie mit Hilfe der Verteilungsfunktion \(\phi\) (Tabelle) die folgenden Wahrscheinlichkeiten: 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 494, 735, 512]]<|/det|>
a) \(P(U \ge 0,95)\) b) \(P(U \ge -2,13)\) c) \(P(-1 \le U \le 1)\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 511, 490, 529]]<|/det|>
d) \(P(X \le 1,72)\) e) \(P(X \le -0,35)\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 545, 147, 562]]<|/det|>
a) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 560, 715, 578]]<|/det|>
\[ P(U \ge 0,95) = 1 - P(U \le 0,95) = 1 - \phi(0,95) = 1 - 0,8289 = 0,1711 \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 579, 147, 596]]<|/det|>
b) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 595, 580, 613]]<|/det|>
\[ P(U \ge -2,13) = 1 - P(U \le -2,13) = 1 - \phi(-2,13) = \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[245, 618, 563, 636]]<|/det|>
\[ = 1 - 1 + \phi(2,13) = \phi(2,13) = 0,9834 \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 637, 147, 654]]<|/det|>
c) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 653, 735, 671]]<|/det|>
\[ P(-1 \le U \le 1) = P(|U| \le 1) = 2 \cdot \phi(1) - 1 = 2 \cdot 0,8413 - 1 = 0,6826 \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 673, 147, 690]]<|/det|>
d) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 689, 420, 708]]<|/det|>
\[ P(X \le 1,72) = \Phi(1,72) = 0,9573 \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 710, 147, 727]]<|/det|>
e) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 726, 690, 768]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} P(X \le -0,35) &= P(X > 0,35) = 1 - P(X \le 0,35) = 1 - \Phi(0,35) \\ &= 1 - 0,6368 = 0,3632, \end{aligned} \]
