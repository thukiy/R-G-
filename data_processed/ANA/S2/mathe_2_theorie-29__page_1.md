<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 52, 380, 78]]<|/det|>

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[78, 90, 211, 115]]<|/det|>
\[ \epsilon \int 1 \, dA = A \]

## Integration über Gebiete: 

<|ref|>image<|/ref|><|det|>[[78, 238, 420, 383]]<|/det|>
 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 394, 365, 437]]<|/det|>
\[ \int_G f \, dA = \int_a^b \int_{g(x)}^{h(x)} f(x, y) \, dy \, dx \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 477, 840, 641]]<|/det|>
\[ \int_G f(x, y) \, dA = \int_c^d \int_{g(y)}^{h(y)} f(x, y) \, dx \, dy \quad \text{Normalbereich II} \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 655, 410, 708]]<|/det|>
\[ \int_G f(x, y) \, dA = \int_a^b \int_{g(y)}^{h(y)} f(x, y) \, dx \, dy + \int_c^d \int_{g(y)}^{h(y)} f(x, y, z) \, dz \, dy \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 750, 420, 884]]<|/det|>
\[ \int_G f(x, y) \, dA = \int_a^{b} \int_c^{g(x)} f(x, y) \, dy \, dx + \int_c^{d} \int_{g(y)}^{h(y)} f(x, y, z) \, dz \]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[52, 896, 425, 940]]<|/det|>
\[ \int_G f(x, y) \, dA = \int_a^{\infty} \int_c^{\infty} f(x, y) \, dy \, dx \]
