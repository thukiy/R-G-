# Mengenlehre 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[250, 115, 627, 147]]<|/det|>
**Satz 1.9** Seien A, B und C Mengen. Dann gelten die 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[261, 182, 405, 210]]<|/det|>
1. Assoziativgesetze: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[414, 240, 610, 310]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} A \cup (B \cup C) &= (A \cup B) \cup C, \\ A \cap (B \cap C) &= (A \cap B) \cap C. \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[261, 360, 410, 388]]<|/det|>
2. Distributivgesetze: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[387, 408, 633, 479]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} A \cap (B \cup C) &= (A \cap B) \cup (A \cap C), \\ A \cup (B \cap C) &= (A \cup B) \cap (A \cup C). \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[261, 530, 421, 558]]<|/det|>
3. Kommutativgesetze: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[452, 588, 570, 658]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} A \cap B &= B \cap A, \\ A \cup B &= B \cup A. \end{aligned} \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[261, 710, 374, 738]]<|/det|>
4. Transitivit√§t: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[406, 740, 617, 770]]<|/det|>
\[ A \subset B \text{ und } B \subset C \implies A \subset C. \]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[261, 808, 682, 840]]<|/det|>
5. Falls \(B, C \subset A\), dann gelten die DE MORGANschen Regeln: 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[397, 867, 626, 940]]<|/det|>
\[ \begin{aligned} A \setminus (B \cap C) &= (A \setminus B) \cup (A \setminus C), \\ A \setminus (B \cup C) &= (A \setminus B) \cap (A \setminus C). \end{aligned} \]
