### 1. Standardnormalverteilung 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 408, 816, 444]]<|/det|>
U sei eine standardnormalverteilte Zufallsvariable. Berechnen Sie mit Hilfe der Verteilungsfunktion \(\phi(u)\) (Tabelle) die folgenden Wahrscheinlichkeiten: 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 443, 748, 462]]<|/det|>
a) \(P(U \le 1,52)\) b) \(P(U \le -0,42)\) c) \(P(0,2 \le U \le 2,13)\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[115, 460, 707, 479]]<|/det|>
d) \(P(-1,01 \le U \le -0,25)\) e) \(P(-1 \le U \le 1)\) f) \(P(|U| \le 1,69)\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 494, 463, 512]]<|/det|>
\[a) P(U \le 1,52) = \phi(1,52) = 0,9357\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 518, 760, 537]]<|/det|>
\[b) P(U \le -0,42) = \phi(-0,42) = 1 - \phi(0,42) = 1 - 0,6628 = 0,3372\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 543, 768, 562]]<|/det|>
\[c) P(0,2 \le U \le 2,13) = \phi(2,13) - \phi(0,2) = 0,9834 - 0,5793 = 0,4041\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 568, 630, 586]]<|/det|>
\[d) P(-1,01 \le U \le -0,25) = \phi(-0,25) - \phi(-1,01) =\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[157, 591, 867, 610]]<|/det|>
\[= 1 - \phi(0,25) - 1 + \phi(1,01) = \phi(1,01) - \phi(0,25) = 0,8438 - 0,5987 = 0,2451\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 616, 833, 636]]<|/det|>
\[e) P(-1 \le U \le 1) = P(|U| \le 1) = 2 \cdot \phi(1) - 1 = 2 \cdot 0,8413 - 1 = 0,6826\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 641, 707, 661]]<|/det|>
\[f) P(|U| \le 1,69) = 2 \cdot \phi(1,69) - 1 = 2 \cdot 0,9545 - 1 = 0,9090\]
