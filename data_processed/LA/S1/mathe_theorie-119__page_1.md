Geomehische Deutung 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 50, 636, 80]]<|/det|>
\(\vec{v}\) und \(\vec{w}\) spannen ein Parallelogramm auf 

<|ref|>image<|/ref|><|det|>[[180, 94, 384, 188]]<|/det|>
 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[460, 123, 585, 160]]<|/det|>
\[\frac{h}{|\vec{v}|} = \sin \varphi\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[129, 207, 512, 233]]<|/det|>
Rächeninhalt Parallelogramm : 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[150, 243, 720, 272]]<|/det|>
\[A = |\vec{v}| \cdot h = |\vec{v}| \cdot |\vec{w}| \cdot \sin \varphi = |\vec{v} \times \vec{w}|\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[18, 300, 430, 340]]<|/det|>
Bsp : \(\vec{v} = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{2}{-2} \\ -2 \end{pmatrix}\) \(\vec{w} = \begin{pmatrix} \frac{3}{5} \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[135, 360, 835, 450]]<|/det|>
\[\vec{v} \times \vec{w} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 5 \end{pmatrix} \times \begin{pmatrix} 1 \cdot 5 - (-2) \cdot 0 \\ (-2) \cdot 3 - 2 \cdot 5 \\ 2 \cdot 0 - 1 \cdot 3 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ -16 \\ -3 \end{pmatrix}\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[135, 456, 820, 490]]<|/det|>
\[|\vec{v} \times \vec{w}| = \sqrt{5^2 + (-16)^2 + (-3)^2} = \sqrt{290} \approx 17,03\]

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[18, 517, 220, 546]]<|/det|>
Rechenregeln : 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 555, 795, 588]]<|/det|>
a) \(\vec{u} \times (\vec{v} + \vec{w}) = \vec{u} \times \vec{v} + \vec{u} \times \vec{w}\) distributiv 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 593, 880, 625]]<|/det|>
b) \(\vec{v} \times \vec{w} = -\vec{w} \times \vec{v}\) anti-kommutativ 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 632, 800, 666]]<|/det|>
c) \(\lambda (\vec{v} \times \vec{w}) = (\lambda \vec{v}) \times \vec{w} = \vec{v} \times (\lambda \vec{w})\) \(\lambda \in \mathbb{R}\) 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 690, 870, 722]]<|/det|>
→ \((\vec{u} \times \vec{v}) \times \vec{w} \neq \vec{u} \times (\vec{v} \times \vec{w})\) nicht assoziativ 

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[46, 752, 530, 785]]<|/det|>
→ \(\vec{v} \times \vec{w} = 0\), \(\vec{v}, \vec{w} \neq 0\) 

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[105, 792, 650, 823]]<|/det|>
\[|\vec{v}| \cdot |\vec{w}| \cdot \sin \varphi = 0 \iff \sin \varphi = 0\]

<|ref|>equation<|/ref|><|det|>[[544, 833, 928, 863]]<|/det|>
\[\varphi = 0^\circ \text{ oder } \varphi = 180^\circ \triangleq \pi\]

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[123, 870, 725, 910]]<|/det|>
⇒ \(\vec{v}\) und \(\vec{w}\) sind parallel bzw. antiparallel
